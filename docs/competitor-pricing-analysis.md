# Eventune Studios - Competitor Pricing Analysis & Recommendation

## Executive Summary

Based on comprehensive market research, the custom song industry has established pricing between **$139-$250** for standard songs. To capture market share, Eventune should enter **20-30% below** competitors while leveraging its unique Afrobeat positioning and the underserved African market.

### African Market Gap
**Key Finding:** No dedicated custom song service targets Cameroon/West Africa with XAF pricing. This is a major opportunity.

---

## Recommended Pricing Strategy

### Philosophy: "Premium Quality, Accessible Price"
- **Entry price 25% below Songlorious** ($45 → $35)
- **Standard price 20% below Songfinch** ($199 → $159)
- **Aggressive XAF pricing** for African market penetration
- **Video add-ons as profit center** (50%+ margins)

## Competitive Advantages

1. **Afrobeat Specialization** - No competitor focuses on African genres
2. **XAF Pricing** - First to serve Cameroon/West Africa directly
3. **Video Bundles** - Competitors charge separately or don't offer
4. **Lower Entry Point** - $35 vs. $45+ competitors
5. **Transparent Pricing** - No hidden fees, clear add-on structure

## Price Increase Roadmap

### Phase 1: Launch (Months 1-6)
- Current pricing as listed above
- Goal: 100+ orders, build reviews

### Phase 2: Growth (Months 7-12)
- Increase all prices by 10-15%
- Example: Single $99 → $109-$114
- Justify with portfolio/testimonials

### Phase 3: Established (Year 2+)
- Match or exceed competitor pricing
- Example: Single $99 → $149-$179
- Premium positioning with proven quality
