# RISK ANALYSIS

## Key Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Likelihood | Impact | Mitigation |
|------|------------|--------|------------|
| Low initial sales | Medium | High | Pre-launch audience building |
| Quality complaints | Low | High | Robust QC process, revisions policy |
| Payment fraud | Low | High | Webhook verification, amount validation |
| Competitor copying | Medium | Medium | Build brand, community, relationships |
| Burnout | Medium | High | Capacity limits, sustainable pace |
| Tech failure | Low | High | Supabase Pro (backups), monitoring |

## Capacity Management

| Your Capacity | Orders/Month | Strategy |
|---------------|--------------|----------|
| Part-time (15-20 hrs) | 12-18 | Focus on Classic package |
| Full-time (40 hrs) | 25-35 | All packages |
